ABOUT US

Welcome to Bookrary, your digital space for discovering, sharing, and enjoying books anytime, anywhere.

Some detail about us: We are Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology students, our team members also from different department, Computer Science and Telecommunication & Networking department.

MISSION

Our mission is to make reading more accessible by connecting readers with books in a simple and modern way.

VISION

We believe that knowledge should be available for everyone. Bookrary aims to create a community where readers and learners can grow together.

From easy-to-use search tools to personalized reading recommendations, Bookrary is designed to make your reading journey enjoyable. Bookrarystarted as a simple idea: to bring people closer to books they love. What began as a project is now growing into a platform that connects readers worldwide. We invite you to explore Bookrary and become part of our story. Let’s keep the joy of reading alive—together!
